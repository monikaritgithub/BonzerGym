How Gym Training Works

Never feel confused again. We break down the levels so you understand exactly what to do and why it is vital to your progress.

Level 01

Beginner Guide

  • Master exercise forms and machine adjustments
  • Focus on full-body stability and stamina
  • 3 sessions per week (30-45 minutes)

Why it's necessary: Builds neuromuscular pathways, strengthens connective tissues, and establishes consistent training habits safely.

Level 02

Intermediate Guide

  • Focus on progressive overload and heavier weights
  • Dedicated splits (Push-Pull-Legs or Upper-Lower)
  • Body recomposition and hypertrophy focus

Why it's necessary: Prevents initial plateaus, challenges muscles to grow larger, and adapts to higher training volume.

Level 03

Advanced Guide

  • Targeted muscle group isolation & shaping
  • High intensity (supersets, dropsets, negatives)
  • Optimized recovery and nutrition integration

Why it's necessary: Maximizes genetic potential, breaks through stubborn plateaus, and builds peak physical condition.
